What We Do

BRE specializes in the rapid development of solutions for its customers in the defense and intelligence communities.

National defense priorities require rapid solution development, bringing best-of-breed techniques to bear in record time.

A sample of our expertise:

  • High Performance Computing (HPC) Architecture Design
  • X-midas Application Development
  • Signal Processing / DSP, including the detection and characterization of LPI / LPD signals
  • Software-defined radio (SDR)
  • GPUs / CUDA
  • Algorithm design, acceleration, and optimization
  • High-speed data aquisition and storage
  • Rapid Protoyping

Who We Are

Blue Ridge Envisioneering, Inc. is a small business incorporated under the laws of the Commonwealth of Virginia. Our principal employees have over 50 years combined experience serving our Defense and Intelligence communities. We innovate solutions when larger corporations cannot.

Our team of seasoned professionals understand our customer’s environment and challenges, and have a broad technical background with skills that span the physical sciences and multiple engineering disciplines.

We use our combination of experience and technical breadth and depth to develop novel and visionary solutions for our customers. We work closely with our customers to understand their needs, then apply best practices from relevant areas to innovate and develop best-of-breed solutions.
